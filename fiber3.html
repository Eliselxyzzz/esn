DTS optical Fiber distributed temperature measurement system

DTS optical Fiber distributed temperature measurement system is the use of Fiber optic sensing signal and the transmission signal as well as the use of advanced technology and the Raman scattered light OTDR temperature sensitive properties, detect different positions along the optical Fiber temperature changes, according to intelligence data analysis, achieve truly distributed real-time online monitoring and timely warning of fire hazards and pinpoint the location of fires.
Advantages
  1. With a real-time online, high sensitivity, high accuracy, high reliability
  2. With the intrinsically safe, anti-electromagnetic interference and lightning protection requirements, environmental adaptability
  3. The measurement distance for remote real-time online monitoring
  4. Long life, simple system

Applications
  1. Cable tray. Cable tunnel, cable trench, high-voltage cables
  2. Storage tanks, gas tank, oil pipelines, gas pipelines
  3. Tunnel, subway tunnels, dams
  4. Transmission belts, coal trestle, coal yard
  5. Fiber optic distributed temperature measurement system can also be used arsenal, dangerous goods storage, grain storage, dams and other space required continuous temperature measurement occasions, the traditional temperature measurement methods (temperature cables, Thermocouple) ideal upgrade alternatives

The System Components

System monitoring software, demodulation analysis instrument, temperature cables and auxiliary equipment (industrial host and monitor, backup power, cabinets, alarms, etc.).
