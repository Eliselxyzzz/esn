What We have Achieved

With the demands of Customer requirements and System specifications, our solutions/products are designed to be in compliance with High and Low temperature operational tolerance, withstanding degrees of vibration, electrostatic radiated interference, while meeting harsh operating environment and applications. Together with our customers, we collaborate with several organisations delivering couple of national key projects in the area of research and development.

Our other achievements include online monitoring system research and development of products, such as Fiber Bragg grating and DTS, widely used in the distributed optical Fiber temperature system. Our products serve to provide the advantages of optical Fiber sensing with safety monitoring methodology; for the dam of civil, bridge and tunnel structure safety and electric power distribution grid, metallurgy, petroleum, petrochemical, coal, gas industry. The FOD foreign body detection system for civil airports and security used by the military airport runway falls under one of our core competencies.
